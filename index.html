Gaining experience and knowledge during the Computer Aided Design and Drafting program at Portland Community College has been a great experience. I am adept at learning new technologies and look forward to learning more Cad programs. I have drafting skills, including making 2-D mechanical drawings using hand sketching techniques while applying ANSI best practices.

Currently I am taking classes on Digital Design and Fabrication, Statics and Mechanics for Mechanical Designers, Materials and Design for Manufacturing Processes, and AutoDesk Inventor Fundamentals. I am also interested in civil engineering and in later semesters will take AutoCad and possibly Intro to Revit and Introduction to Architectural Drawing.

I also have general skills that are essential for a CAD drafter. I am a detail-oriented person who strives to present the best possible product. I am good at working with a team and can follow directions from different people. Overall, I am a hard-working person who is willing to go above and beyond for his team.
