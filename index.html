Hello!

I'm Darren, a Portland native and web dev enthusiast. I like to create and fix stuff. Finding a solution to a tough problem is one of the most satisfying things one could do and my competitive and patient nature help to keep me working at the problem until it's solved.

I make apps using React.js, Redux, or jQuery for the front-end and Node.js with MongoDB or PostgreSQL for the back-end. I would love to create games in genres I think could use a different angle. I have many ideas and look forward to being part of a great team.

Outside of the work I spend most of my time with my wife and daughter. Cooking, reading to my daughter and hiking. In my spare time I like to play the bass guitar, board games, video games, soccer, basketball or racquetball.
