I'm Darren, a web developer and Portland native. I like to create and fix stuff. Finding a solution to a tough problem is one of the most satisfying things one could do, and my patient and competitive nature help to keep me working at the problem until it's solved.

I make apps using React.js, Redux, or jQuery for the front-end, and Node.js with MongoDB or PostgreSQL for the back-end. I enjoy the challenge of working on full stack applications and I love the open and ever changing web dev community. I have many ideas and look forward to being part of a great team.

Outside of work I spend time with my wife and daughter and we enjoy cooking, reading, and hiking. In my spare time I like to play the bass guitar, board games, video games, indoor soccer, basketball, or racquetball.
